About Me

Philip Kuo is a high school student with innovative ideas and passionate dreams. He started to self-learn web-developing skills at an early age, managed to create his own tech blog when in third grade, and launched his first personal website at an age of 9. Philip spent one year in the US as an exchange student after he graduated from middle school, he became more independent, self-motivated and innovative after then. While he had to deal with cultural shock and the language barrier, his passion was still burning. Philip shared his personal experience in his blog “Phil’s America Exchange Year”, and developed a mobile app for a better blog reading experience on mobile phones. He is a participant of many conferences such as Global Initiatives Symposium in Taiwan, he is also an active MUNer. Philip recently formed a school wide broadcast news station in his current high school. Being a high school student in Taiwan might be stressful, but his passion of fulfilling his dream makes everything possible. Just as the saying goes, “What the mind can conceive, humankind can achieve.”
