Kitzsteinhorn

Website Relaunch

PURE GLACIAL ENJOYMENT

Skiers and boarders experience pure freedom on the Kitzsteinhorn. Beautiful wide glacial slopes make the hearts of snow freaks beat faster. Expressing these feelings into a new complex website was the biggest challenge for this project.

We built a social hub that pulls in all conversations from different social media channels about Kitzsteinhorn to combine them in one functional place.
— Johannes Klaushofer, Development

Structure

As the website contains a significant number of complex tools and modules, defining the perfect structure was key for a successful relaunch.

Information

When defining the goals for the website, we clearly set the focus on usability. That way users can find the information they're looking for within a few clicks.

Emotion

The images provided by the client, shot by our friends from ideenwerk have been the perfect basis to create a eye-catching and emotional approach.

The Beauty of Kitzsteinhorn

How to build a new website for a beautiful ski resort? Well, it was all about eye-catching large scale visuals to express the feeling of being closer than ever before.

CUISINE

„Enjoyment at every level“ is the motto of the Kitzsteinhorn gastronomy. Varied and colourful cuisine from the valley right up to the Gipfelstation at 3029 m – never boring!

Snowpark

Massive airtime, endless jibbing fun and creative setups in three Snowparks, as well as a huge superpipe, a railgarden in the centre of Kaprun and heaps of events.
